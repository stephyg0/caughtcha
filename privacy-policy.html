Caughtcha Privacy Policy

Caughtcha does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

The extension may locally store user preferences such as blocked websites and settings in order to provide its functionality.

Caughtcha only accesses website activity required to detect and interrupt distracting websites selected by the user.

No personally identifiable information is collected or sold.
